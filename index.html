Our Story

Wassim Serhan and Heather Falconer met in July 2021 at Lake Street bar in Greenpoint whilst watching the England and Italy football game. England may have lost, but Wassim and Heather certainly won. The pair have been inseparable from that moment onwards. They have taken trips to the countryside of Upstate New York, Chicago, Florida, Sweden, England and Mexico.

Fun fact: Wassim and Heather have been at many of the same events, and share friends, yet never met until 2021.
